Learn Chinese Today

今天
/ jīntiān /
Today
今天我去学校。
Today I go to school. (Hôm nay tôi đi học.)
💡 Memory Tip
今 (jīn) means "now" and 天 (tiān) means "sky/day." Together they represent "this day" or "today." Think of it as the sky (heaven) of the present moment.

Autumn Whispers
5 min read
Progress:
40%

The leaves fell in a gentle cascade, each one a small farewell to the season. Outside my window, the world was painting itself in shades of amber and gold. I watched, mesmerized, as the wind orchestrated a silent symphony.

There is something profoundly peaceful about autumn. It is the season that teaches us the beauty of letting go, of releasing what no longer serves us. The trees do not mourn their fallen leaves; they accept the cycle, knowing that renewal lies dormant beneath.

I thought of the conversations I had yet to have, the stories left untold. Perhaps they too were like these leaves—meant to fall away naturally, to make space for something new. Perhaps acceptance was the greatest form of courage.
